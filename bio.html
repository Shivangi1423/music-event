BIOGRAPHY

Atif Aslam born in Wazirabad, Gujranwala District, Punjab, Pakistan as Muhammad Atif Aslam is a Pakistani pop singer and film actor. His debut as an actor was in the 2011 film Pakistani movie “Bol”. Atif Aslam’s soulful voice crossed the Pakistani border and reached India when Bollywood director Mahesh Bhatt called Atif Aslam to seek his consent to put “Woh Lamhey” as a song in the soundrack of his new motion picture, Zeher. After that, Atif Aslam’s music spread across to people and radio stations that play it all over the subcontinent now.
